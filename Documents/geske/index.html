Teaching Research Miscellaneous

Welcome!

My name is Christian Geske (he/him/his) and I am an Assistant Teaching Professor in Mathematics at the University of Southern California, where I am also faculty mentor for the SC Mathematics Club and am responsible for coordinating the Undergraduate Seminar Talks.

Background

I was born in Vienna, Austria but was soon brought by my American parents to the United States, where I grew up in Greenwich, Connecticut. I did my BS in Mathematics at the University of Southern California from 2010–2014, then transitioned to University of Wisconsin–Madison where I completed my PhD in Mathematics from 2014–2019 under the advisement of Laurentiu Maxim. Afterwards, I was a Postdoctoral Lecturer at Northwestern University, an Adjunct Instructor at Saint Louis University, and a short–term teacher at John Burroughs School in Saint Louis.
